Our Humble Beginnings

The Bellur Utensil Company was started in 1948 in Middlefield, Massachusetts by Sara Bellur. Sara had a dram to create the best cooking utensils possible, and who better to create that product, that the mother of eight. Sara was a homemaker, and spent much of her time creating fabulous feasts for her growing family. Using birch wood, Sara carved paddles and spoons and sold them to the local general store and farm stands.

Sara's products boomed, and soon her products went from being a local item to a regional item, and then as they say,"the rest is history."Today the company is still family-owned with the great-grandchildren of Sara Bellur, Charles Bellur and Danica Roth currently managing the business

In 1970, the growing company moved to its present location in Newport, Connecticut. The product line was expanded, and modern materials were incorporated into the construction of the utensils.

Today all Bellur Utensils are engineered with care and produced with high-tech materials. Bellur Utensils is the longest lasting, and easiest brand of utensils to use in the world.
