Employment Options

Spoon Engineer:

Responsibilities for this position include designing and overseeing production of new spoons. Bachelor of Science in Utensil Technology is required. Experience with polymers and metalworking is a plus.

Test Kitchen Attendant:

Responsibilities for this position include preparation of food as directed by Test Kitchen Head Chef, maintenance of kitchen equipment, and cleaning of kitchen. High school diploma required. College degree is a plus.

Sales Representative:

Responsibilities for this position include finding and following sales leads in the southern Alabama region. Company car provided. College degree and 5 years of sales experience required.

Receptionist:

Responsibilities for this position include greeting guests, answering the phone, filing, data entry, and other miscellaneous duties as needed. Professional attire and computer skills are required.

Web Designer:

Responsibilities for this position include developing, supporting, and maintaining the company's website. Proficient skills in web design software and photo editing software are required.
